# Limpack

## Requirements

- Minecraft JE 1.21.2 \(or greater\)

## Using namespace

lim

## Effects

- Netherite Tools & Armors Crafting.
- Chainmail Armors Crafting \(using chains\).
- Craft Gilding Tools & Armors
- Mine copper, iron, gold and lapis lazuli \(netherite\) drops their ingot, not their raw ore.
- Kill animals drops their cooked meat, not raw meat.
- In crafting table, an ingot + \(white \| yellow \| orange \| black\) dye =&gt; \(iron \| gold \| copper \| netherite \) ingot.
- In crafting table, \(iron \| gold \| copper \| netherite \) ingot =&gt; \(white \| yellow \| orange \| black\) dyes * 9
- 100% ender pearls on piglin bartering
- In crafting table, water bucket + lava bucket =&gt; 1 obsidian

## Structures

Please use `place template`.

```
lim:skyblock/single
lim:skyblock/default
lim:skyblock/grid
```
